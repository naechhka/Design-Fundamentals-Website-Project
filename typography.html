Axial

For this system, I divided text using imaginary lines which are slightly angled.

Radial

For radial type, I chose a circle as the central focal point and located all text around it, using lines to divide the content into groups.

Dilatation

For this system, I decided to place text along circle. I continued use the same shade of red as in the previous types, but I changed the opacity.

Random

In this system, I placed all shapes and text in random positions and colours.

Grid

For grid, I imagined dividing the canvas into 4 rows and 2 columns, where I aligned content on each row, alternating first and second column.

Modular

For modular system, I chose rectangles and squares as a repeatable structure. I divided content into groups and placed in each rectangle.

Transitional

I found this system the most challenging to implement. I decided to create and an imitation of waves movement, by arranging the text in the form of waves.

Bilateral

For the last system, I created a kind of axis, using lines, that divided the canvas into two equal parts, and then placed the text between them symmetrically.

HI! I’m Anastasiia, though friends just call me Nastya, originally from Ukraine by the way. At 19, I stepped into my first year Digital media at UWE. Funny enough, once I said, "I will never do websites again in my life”, but now here I am, presenting my website for Graphic & Web design studio, and I actually enjoy it.

Reflection

I began my journey at UWE in 2023 with a foundation year. During that time, I struggled with a module that was quite similar to Graphic & Web Design Studio. However, the first assignment that we have on GWDS seemed more achievable than on foundation year. To approach this assignment, I divided the whole project into five components: worksheets, CARP task, typography, web building and writing.

Firstly, I started from worksheets, and they were not that complicated like I thought before. With prior experience in web building, I found coding sheets useful to refresh my skills in HTML and CSS again. As concerns GitHub, I had often heard about it but had not used it before this module and it turned out to be a free way to host static websites using repositories.

Then, almost at the same time with worksheets I initiated to do my CARP posters which were difficult in the beginning because of lack of knowledge in InDesign. However, after several attempts to do first poster I gained confidence. Then I faced the problem, that I did not find inspiration every time I tried to do posters for True/False film festival, I forced myself to do it at the time when I needed to, but that was ineffective. Therefore, when ideas came to my mind, I started to implement them immediately. All posters were going to be completely different, and I achieved that.

As regards typography task, I completed it faster than posters, although, I did not have experience in Illustrator, acquired skills in InDesign helped to understand how this software works. To be honest, I have done all 8 types at one day but then changed them multiple times because they did not fit my webpage.

The most significant and important part was building of the web site on which I spent more time than on other sections. Firstly, I started from creating design for my web pages, I have couple of hand-drawn sketches just for a layout and some on my tablet where I was doing more detailed. Equally important is colour pallete, which I was choosing for almost whole day and changed it for about 30 times. As a basis, I stopped at three main colours: navy blue, a shade of red and milky colour. The final website closely matches my vision, however, there is one aspect which I do not like about my web pages is how certain pages look on different screen sizes.

Final task was to complete writing part. During web building process I used Lorem Ipsum to visualise, how each page looks with content on it. Once coding was finished, I replaced Lorem Ipsum with required content. In conclusion, I am pleased with the work I have accomplished although it has some imperfections. This assignment improved my skills in web building and graphic design, and I am looking forward for next project.
